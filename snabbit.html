Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Snabbit home services.

Snabbit is India's first on-demand home services platform. We provide quick and reliable female House Help at your doorstep within 10 minutes.
Download the Snabbit app, select your service, choose "Instant Booking", and a verified Expert will be dispatched — typically arriving within 10 minutes.
Our experts handle dishwashing, kitchen cleaning, bathroom cleaning, fan and window cleaning, laundry help, and more. You can bundle multiple tasks in one booking.
Every Expert undergoes Aadhaar + PAN verification, a multi-step background check, and 2 days of professional training before their first booking.
You can cancel or reschedule through the app before the Expert is dispatched. Please refer to our cancellation policy in the app for details on applicable charges.
For instant bookings the average arrival time is 10 minutes. You can also schedule bookings in advance for a specific time that suits your routine.
